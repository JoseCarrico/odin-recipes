Francesinha

Description

The francesinha in its special sandwich variation is more commonly made up of sausage, fresh sausage, ham, cold meats and beef steak, topped with later melted cheese.

Ingridients

  • Loaf bread;
  • Ham;
  • Sausage;
  • Stake;
  • Salt;
  • Butter;
  • Eggs;
  • Cheese;
  • Francesinha sauce;

Steps

  1. Toast the loaf bread and cut the sausage in half;
  2. Season the stake and put it on the ffrying pan with butter;
  3. Now build the francesinha in the following order;
    1. loaf bread;
    2. ham;
    3. sausage;
    4. stake;
    5. loaf bread;
    6. cheese;
  4. Pour the francesinha sauce above teh francesinha and take it to the oven for 5 minutes;
  5. Fry one egg and put it above the francesinha;
