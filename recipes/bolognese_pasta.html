Bolognese Pasta

Description

This recipe, originally from the Italian city of Bologna, is one of the most appreciated pastes in the world. Simple and quick, you only need 30 minutes to prepare it. Gather the family and bring them to the table.

Ingridients

  • Spaghetti;
  • Garlic;
  • Onion;
  • Olive oil;
  • Minced meat;
  • Salt;
  • Tomato;
  • Grated cheese;

Steps

  1. Cook teh spaghetti
  2. On the fry pan put the garlic, onion and the olive oil;
  3. Gather the minced meat
  4. mix all together
  5. Gather with the cooked spaghetti and the grated cheese
